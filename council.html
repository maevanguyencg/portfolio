The Council: Expected Freedom

Pitch & Keypoints

2204: our world is devastated after an unprecedented economic and sanitary crisis. The current government just seized control forcefully and is enslaving the population. Former guard of those rulers, you’re now an investigator working for the rebellion. With your heavy address book, your maps of sensitive places, your computer, your full access to all types of news and your research capacities, resolve the enquiries that awaits you to dismantle the politic power! Will you be able to free the people from tyranny? Can you find out why you were fired? Can you steal the needed information to help the rebels? Will you risk your life to recruit new agents? Start your mission now!

  • - Post-apocalyptic world based on our own civilization
  • - Investigation and deduction game
  • - Multiple elements inside the box as cards, newspapers, address books, maps...
  • - Multiple choices to resolve the investigation
  • - Side and optional quests
  • - 10 main cases that follow each other
  • - Language: English
