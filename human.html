First Human

Pitch & Keypoints

The enchanted world is dying. Elves, Orcs, Werewolves, Dwarves and other magical creatures are losing their powers and nothing seems to be of any help. But in this tragedy, a soul is born, the first human. You incarnate this precursor, eager to live and to find your place among the older races. Travel all over the world, meet extraordinary people, fight against your worse nightmares, learn the secrets of your past and train for your future! A whole civilization awaits to be built!

  • - Fantasy world
  • - One player game
  • - Linear story with descriptions, dialogues and multiple NPCs (Non-Playable Characters)
  • - Game Design creation
  • - Language: English

Description (project)

First Human is an interactive video game where all of your choices matter for the story and your progression. You play the first representative of humanity who must build the entire civilization.

The game is a mix between an RPG and an STR where you must both master your character and plan for the future of mankind.

During your adventure, you will meet other species and you should strengthen your relationships with them, learn how to survive in various biomes, build shelters and tools, hunt, but also fight to stay alive, conquest strategic places and protect your fellow souls.

World

The game starts in a Fantasy universe populated with enchanted creatures as elves, werewolves, orcs, centaurs, dwarves and so on. But the wells of magic are emptying and the supernatural beings get weaker and weaker. In this context, some babies born powerless and with different physical shapes. It is your case, as a human born among the elves. You are rejected by your tribe and seen as an abnormality. But you soon discover that you are a perfect fit for this new magicless world.
