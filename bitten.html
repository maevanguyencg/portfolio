Bitten!

Pitch

During a hunt, Thomas is bitten by a werewolf and inevitably becomes one himself. Eager to find a cure, he faces countless challenges and disappointments. But a greater Devil grows inside the human world and Thomas must stay hopeful and keep control to save his family.

  • - Medieval Dark Fantasy (based on actual history)
  • - Linear heavy-narrative game
  • - Story in 5 acts
  • - Historical research, games/movies/books references
  • - Creation of the whole universe
  • - Details of a quest
  • - Backstories
  • - Language: English

Chapters of the game

Chapter 1: The Hunter

Thomas is bitten by a werewolf while hunting in the forest. When he goes back to Westwend, he finds it attacked and left by its inhabitants. Thomas must understand what happened and reach his family.

Chapter 2: The Truth

Thomas finds his wife and the rest of the village in Frostford but must stay hidden because of his new nature. He learns about the werewolves by his best friend, Laurens. The Red Knights captured Thomas and experiment a cure using bloody rituals.

Chapter 3: The Hope

Thomas is crafting the Mercy Potion, his best hope for a cure. At the same time, he learns a lot about the Red Knights and their plans.

Chapter 4: The Faith

With the failure of the Mercy Potion, Thomas turns to God. Both Father Vincent and the young doctor Elizabeth De Saint Leu do their best to help the young werewolf. Thomas meets a clan of Druids and must learn how to control his transformations.

Chapter 5: The Prey

Thomas promises to kill each member of the Red Knights. He investigates to find Gilles de Rais and is invited by Bathory for a final confrontation.

Sample (quest)

9 – An unsuspecting meeting

CUTSCENE: While Thomas is looking at the bathroom, someone is coming behind him.

OLIVIER

It is beautiful, isn’t it, big brother?

Thomas turns at once to face his little brother, Olivier.

PLAYABLE CHARACTER

Olivier! You are alive! I can’t believe it, I’m so glad to see you!
But … wait, what did you just say?

Olivier is walking towards Thomas and stops next to him, looking at the bloody pool. He takes a deep breath.

OLIVIER

I know you love blood… The smell of it, and its taste… I know what kind of killer you are, Thomas.

PLAYABLE CHARACTER

What the hell are you talking about? What happened to you? I don’t recognize you … you … you look so different!

OLIVIER

Me? I look different? Haha! I can’t believe I’m hearing this from someone like you!

PLAYABLE CHARACTER

What do you mean, someone like me?

OLIVIER

Oh! You think I don’t know who you are, werewolf?

Thomas looks surprised to learn his brother knows, and before he could answer, Olivier continues.

OLIVIER

I know what kind of monster is hidden inside your very self. I know the beast you became… You, who never seemed to want a complicated life, always refusing any travel or change to your wife… But I should have known what you were hidden from us, what you were hidden from me! All those years hiding yourself in the woods to kill innocent people!
